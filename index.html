Exploring the Original Scriptures

This project is dedicated to investigating what the Bible really says — from its roots in Judaism through early Christianity — by looking at the original Hebrew, Aramaic, and Greek texts.

Many modern doctrines about Hell, God’s character, and the end times are based on mistranslations, church politics, or cultural reinterpretations. Here, we go back to the source.

Why This Matters

  • To distinguish Sheol, Hades, Gehenna, Lake of Fire, and other concepts improperly merged into "Hell"
  • To understand the original meanings of repentance, judgment, mercy, and salvation
  • To shed light on how early Christian thought emerged from Jewish beliefs
  • To explore the true character of God across scripture without distortion

Upcoming Topics

  • Sheol vs. Hades: Peaceful sleep or fiery torment?
  • Gehenna: Real place or metaphor?
  • The Lake of Fire: Final death or eternal torture?
  • How Christianity Changed from Judaism: Doctrinal shifts
  • God’s Nature in Hebrew: Chesed, Emunah, Ruach
